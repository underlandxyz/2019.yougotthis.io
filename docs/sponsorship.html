Sponsor the conference

You Got This is an annual, 150+ person conference for junior developers, designers, and their teams to learn how to better support their co-workers. It will cover topics across three themes - learning, living and levelling-up. This isn’t your typical tech conference - we're not talking about technical skills, but everything else needed for a happy, healthy work life. If you're interested in finding out more about our inclusion programme - we have a whole page dedicated to it.

Underland (Wilson & Kevin) are an experienced team who have delivered dozens of developer-focussed events including hackathons and conferences.

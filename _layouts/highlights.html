Highlights

You Got This 2019 took part on January 19th 2019 at the Monzo office in London. We had 195 people turn up - a mix of junior developers and those looking to how they can better support their team members. Thanks to sponsors and those who paid over the recommended ticket price - we were able to offer 97 scholarships to You Got This, including 8 which included travel, accommodation, childcare and a stipend to make participation cost-neutral. Our event retrospective is here.
